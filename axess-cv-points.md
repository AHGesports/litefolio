## INTERVIEWER'S PICK (What I'd want to see for .NET/Angular role)

As an interviewer for a senior .NET/Angular position at a large company, these points would catch my attention:

### Top Choice (3 bullets)
- Full-stack developer across 5 interconnected enterprise systems using ASP.NET MVC, Angular, Entity Framework, and Oracle databases
- Implemented end-to-end workflows for ticketing, guest services, and accreditation with role-based access control and role-based access control
- Contributed to multi-tenant B2B architecture serving sports venues and event stadiums across multiple markets

### Why these work:
1. **"5 interconnected enterprise systems"** - Shows you handle complexity, not just CRUD apps
2. **"ASP.NET MVC, Angular, NHibernate, Oracle"** - Exact stack match, proves depth
3. **"End-to-end workflows"** - You understand full business logic, not just UI or API in isolation
4. **"RBAC"** - Authorization architecture, not just "used a library"
5. **"Multi-tenant B2B"** - Architecture understanding, not just feature work
6. **"Sports venues across multiple markets"** - Real-world scale, production systems
